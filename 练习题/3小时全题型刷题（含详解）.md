# Part A · 单项选择题（Single Choice，14×2=28 类比分）

> 全英文，选一个最佳答案。做完看文末统一答案。

1. Which type of question does management accounting answer when it **evaluates how well the organization has performed**?
   A. Attention-directing  B. Scorecard  C. Problem-solving  D. Budgeting

2. Compared with financial accounting, management accounting is **best described as**:
   A. governed by GAAP  B. focused on the past  C. future-oriented and for internal use  D. reported only annually

3. A cost that **remains constant in total** within the relevant range is a:
   A. variable cost  B. fixed cost  C. mixed cost  D. step cost

4. The **unit contribution margin** equals:
   A. selling price − unit variable cost  B. selling price − unit fixed cost
   C. sales − cost of goods sold  D. fixed cost ÷ units

5. A **sunk cost** is:
   A. always relevant  B. a future cost that differs among alternatives
   C. a past cost that is irrelevant to decisions  D. the same as an opportunity cost

6. Selling price $50, unit variable cost $30, fixed costs $100,000. The **break-even point in units** is:
   A. 2,000  B. 3,333  C. 5,000  D. 10,000

7. **Advertising expense** that management can raise or cut each year is a:
   A. committed fixed cost  B. discretionary fixed cost  C. variable cost  D. sunk cost

8. In a **sell-or-process-further** decision, the joint costs incurred before split-off are:
   A. relevant  B. irrelevant  C. opportunity costs  D. variable costs

9. A responsibility center whose manager controls **both revenues and costs but not invested capital** is a:
   A. cost center  B. revenue center  C. profit center  D. investment center

10. Which is **NOT** one of the four perspectives of the **balanced scorecard**?
    A. Financial  B. Customer  C. Competitor  D. Learning and growth

11. The **depreciation tax shield** for $20,000 of depreciation at a 40% tax rate is:
    A. $8,000  B. $12,000  C. $20,000  D. $0

12. Under the **NPV** method, a project should be accepted when:
    A. NPV < 0  B. NPV ≥ 0  C. payback > life  D. IRR < hurdle rate

13. A **flexible budget** is based on:
    A. the planned (master) level of activity  B. the actual level of activity achieved
    C. last year's activity  D. zero activity

14. Special order, idle capacity exists: the order should be accepted if the offered price exceeds the:
    A. full absorption cost  B. total cost per unit
    C. relevant variable cost per unit  D. selling price

<details>
<summary>👉 选择题答案与解析</summary>

| 题 | 答案 | 解析 |
|---|:---:|------|
| 1 | **B** | 评估表现=记分卡问题(scorecard) |
| 2 | **C** | 管理会计：未来导向、内部、不受GAAP约束 |
| 3 | **B** | 总额不变=固定成本 |
| 4 | **A** | 单位贡献毛益=单价−单位变动成本 |
| 5 | **C** | 沉没成本=已发生、不相关 |
| 6 | **C** | 100,000÷(50−30)=5,000 件 |
| 7 | **B** | 可自主增减=酌量性固定成本 |
| 8 | **B** | 联合成本对深加工决策不相关 |
| 9 | **C** | 管收入+成本但不管投资=利润中心 |
| 10 | **C** | 四维度是财务/客户/内部流程/学习成长，没有"竞争者" |
| 11 | **A** | 20,000×40%=8,000 |
| 12 | **B** | NPV≥0 接受 |
| 13 | **B** | 弹性预算按实际业务量编 |
| 14 | **C** | 有闲置产能时，价格>相关变动成本即可接 |

</details>

## 题1（第2章 CVP）

> **Sunshine Co.** sells one product. Selling price **$80**, unit variable cost **$48**, annual fixed costs **$96,000**, expected sales **5,000 units**, income tax rate **25%**.
> Required: (1) unit contribution margin and CM ratio; (2) break-even point in units and in dollars; (3) operating income at expected sales; (4) units needed to earn an **after-tax** profit of **$54,000**; (5) margin of safety in units.

<details>
<summary>👉 题1 解答</summary>

**(1)** Unit CM = 80 − 48 = **$32**；CM ratio = 32 ÷ 80 = **40%**
**(2)** BE units = 96,000 ÷ 32 = **3,000 units**；BE sales = 96,000 ÷ 40% = **$240,000**
**(3)** 贡献式利润表：

| | 金额 |
|---|---|
| Sales 5,000×80 | 400,000 |
| Variable costs 5,000×48 | 240,000 |
| **Contribution margin** | **160,000** |
| Fixed costs | 96,000 |
| **Operating income** | **64,000** |

**(4)** Pre-tax profit = 54,000 ÷ (1−0.25) = 72,000；units = (96,000+72,000) ÷ 32 = 168,000 ÷ 32 = **5,250 units**
**(5)** Margin of safety = 5,000 − 3,000 = **2,000 units**（= $160,000，安全边际率 40%）

**English answer sentence**："The break-even point is 3,000 units ($240,000). At 5,000 units operating income is $64,000. To earn an after-tax profit of $54,000, the company must sell 5,250 units. The margin of safety is 2,000 units."

</details>

## 题2（第5章 贡献式利润表 + 特殊订单）

> **Beta Mfg** has capacity of **50,000 units** and currently produces and sells **40,000 units** at **$25** each. Unit costs: direct materials **$6**, direct labor **$4**, variable manufacturing overhead **$2**, variable selling **$1**; fixed manufacturing overhead **$180,000**, fixed selling & admin **$60,000**.
> Required: (1) prepare a **contribution-format income statement** at 40,000 units; (2) a customer offers to buy **8,000 units at $15** each (no variable selling cost, fixed costs unchanged). Should Beta accept? (3) What is the **minimum acceptable price** for the special order?

<details>
<summary>👉 题2 解答</summary>

**(1)** 单位变动成本(全) = 6+4+2+1 = 13

| | 金额 |
|---|---|
| Sales 40,000×25 | 1,000,000 |
| Variable costs 40,000×13 | 520,000 |
| **Contribution margin** | **480,000** |
| Fixed costs (180,000+60,000) | 240,000 |
| **Operating income** | **240,000** |

**(2)** 闲置产能 = 50,000 − 40,000 = 10,000 ≥ 8,000 ✓（不挤占正常销售）
特殊订单相关单位变动成本 = 6+4+2 = **12**（无变动销售费）
Incremental CM = 8,000 × (15 − 12) = 8,000 × 3 = **+$24,000** → **Accept**（固定成本不变，利润增加 24,000）

**(3)** Minimum price = 相关变动成本 = **$12 / unit**

**English answer**："Beta should accept the special order because it generates an incremental contribution margin of $24,000 and fixed costs are unchanged. The minimum acceptable price is $12, the relevant variable cost per unit."

</details>

## 题3（第6章 自制或外购 + 机会成本）

> **Gamma Co.** needs **10,000 units** of a component. To **make**: direct materials **$5**, direct labor **$4**, variable overhead **$3** per unit; allocated fixed overhead **$50,000**, of which **$15,000 is avoidable** if production stops. To **buy**: **$14** per unit.
> Required: (1) Should Gamma make or buy? (2) If buying lets Gamma use the freed-up facilities to earn a **$12,000** contribution from another product, does the decision change?

<details>
<summary>👉 题3 解答</summary>

**(1)** 只算相关成本（可避免的）：

| | Make | Buy |
|---|---|---|
| 变动成本 10,000×(5+4+3)=12 | 120,000 | |
| 可避免固定成本 | 15,000 | |
| 外购 10,000×14 | | 140,000 |
| **相关成本合计** | **135,000** | **140,000** |

→ **Make**（自制省 $5,000）。注意：50,000 中不可避免的 35,000 两边都有，不相关。

**(2)** 加入机会成本：Buy 的净成本 = 140,000 − 12,000 = **128,000** < Make 135,000 → 改为 **Buy**（决策反转）

**English answer**："Gamma should make the component, since the relevant cost of making ($135,000) is $5,000 less than buying ($140,000). However, if buying frees facilities to earn $12,000 elsewhere, the net cost of buying falls to $128,000, so Gamma should then buy."

</details>

## 题4（第6章 约束资源 + 联产品深加工，两小问）

> **(a)** **Delta Co.** has only **2,400 machine hours** per week. Product **X**: unit contribution margin **$30**, requires **3** machine hours. Product **Y**: unit contribution margin **$24**, requires **2** machine hours. Which product should be given priority?
> **(b)** A joint process yields a batch that can be **sold at split-off for $40,000**, or **processed further at an additional cost of $15,000** and sold for **$50,000**. Sell now or process further?

<details>
<summary>👉 题4 解答</summary>

**(a)** 按**每机器工时贡献毛益**排序：
- X：30 ÷ 3 = **$10/hr**
- Y：24 ÷ 2 = **$12/hr** ← 更高
→ 优先生产 **Y**（虽然 X 单位贡献毛益更高，但每受限工时 Y 赚得多）。

**(b)** 只比分离点之后：
- 增量收入 = 50,000 − 40,000 = 10,000
- 追加加工成本 = 15,000
- 10,000 < 15,000 → **Sell at split-off**（深加工反而亏 5,000）。联合成本不相关。

**English answer**："(a) Product Y should be given priority because its contribution margin per machine hour ($12) exceeds X's ($10). (b) The batch should be sold at split-off, because the incremental revenue of $10,000 is less than the $15,000 additional processing cost."

</details>

## 题5（第11章 资本预算综合：NPV / 回收期 / ARR / 折旧税盾）

> **Epsilon Co.** is considering equipment costing **$200,000**, useful life **5 years**, **no salvage value**, straight-line depreciation. It will generate additional **cash revenue $95,000** and **cash operating costs $30,000** per year. Tax rate **30%**; required rate of return **12%**.
> PV annuity factors (5 years): 12% = **3.6048**, 14% = **3.4331**.
> Required: (1) annual after-tax net cash flow (include the depreciation tax shield); (2) **NPV** — accept or reject? (3) **payback period**; (4) **ARR** based on initial investment; (5) is the **IRR** above or below 12%?

<details>
<summary>👉 题5 解答</summary>

**折旧** = 200,000 ÷ 5 = 40,000 /年

**(1)** 年税后净现金流：
```
税前经营现金流 = 95,000 − 30,000 = 65,000
税后经营现金流 = 65,000 × (1 − 0.30) = 45,500
折旧税盾       = 40,000 × 0.30 = 12,000
年税后净现金流 = 45,500 + 12,000 = $57,500
```

**(2)** NPV = 57,500 × 3.6048 − 200,000 = 207,276 − 200,000 = **+$7,276** > 0 → **Accept**

**(3)** Payback = 200,000 ÷ 57,500 = **3.48 年**

**(4)** ARR（用会计净利润）：
```
税前净利润 = 65,000 − 40,000(折旧) = 25,000
税后净利润 = 25,000 × (1−0.30) = 17,500
ARR(按初始投资) = 17,500 ÷ 200,000 = 8.75%
（若按平均投资 100,000，则 = 17.5%）
```

**(5)** IRR：年金系数 = 200,000 ÷ 57,500 = 3.478，落在 14%(3.4331) 与 12%(3.6048) 之间
→ **IRR ≈ 13.5%，高于 12%** → 与 NPV 结论一致，接受。

**English answer**："The annual after-tax cash flow is $57,500 (= $45,500 operating + $12,000 depreciation tax shield). NPV is +$7,276, so the project should be accepted. The payback period is 3.48 years, the ARR (on initial investment) is 8.75%, and the IRR (≈13.5%) exceeds the 12% required return."

</details>

# Part C · 简答题（Short Answer，英文作答）

> 要点式作答即可，含关键英文术语就给分。先自己写，再对模板。

**Q1.** Define management accounting and state the **three types of questions** it helps answer.
**Q2.** Explain the difference between a **relevant cost** and a **sunk cost**, with one example each.
**Q3.** List the **four perspectives** of the balanced scorecard.

<details>
<summary>👉 简答题模板答案</summary>

**A1.** "Management accounting is the process of **identifying, measuring, and reporting financial and non-financial information** that helps **internal managers make decisions**. It answers three types of questions: **scorecard** (evaluating performance), **attention-directing** (comparing actual with expected results to find problems and opportunities), and **problem-solving** (evaluating alternative courses of action)."

**A2.** "A **relevant cost** is an **expected future cost that differs among alternatives** (e.g., the variable cost of producing a special order). A **sunk cost** is a **past cost that cannot be changed** by any current decision and is therefore **irrelevant** (e.g., the book value of an old machine)."

**A3.** "**Financial; Customer; Internal business process; Learning and growth.**"

</details>
