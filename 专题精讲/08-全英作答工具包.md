# 全英作答工具包（English Answering Toolkit）

> 你最担心的"老师要求英文题英文答"。**别怕——管理会计的英文作答极其套路化**：
> 一道计算题 = **一张带英文标签的表格** + **2~3 句固定句型**。
> 本文给你：① 看懂题干的指令词 ② 答题三段式结构 ③ 表格英文标签 ④ 各题型现成句型 ⑤ 一份完整全英示范答案。
> 把你的数字填进模板即可，不需要自己造句。

---

## 一、先看懂题在问什么：高频"指令词"（command words）

| 英文指令 | 意思 | 你要做什么 |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Compute / Calculate / Determine | 计算 | 列式算出数值 |
| Prepare a … statement/budget/schedule | 编制…表 | 列出标准格式表格 |
| Should the company … ? | 公司是否应该…？ | 做决策并给结论 |
| Explain / Discuss / Why | 解释/讨论/为什么 | 写 1~3 句概念说明 |
| Advise / Recommend | 建议 | 给结论 + 理由 |
| What is the effect of … on … | …对…有何影响 | 算差额并说增/减 |
| Assume / Given that … | 假设/已知 | 这是题目条件 |

> 常见提问骨架：*"Compute the break-even point."* / *"Should ABC accept the special order? Show your analysis."* / *"Prepare the cash budget for June."*

---

## 二、答题三段式结构（万能框架）

任何计算/决策题，英文答案都按这三段写，老师一看就给分：

```
【1. Computation / Analysis】  ← 一张带英文标签的表格 + 算式
【2. Conclusion / Decision】   ← 一句结论句（见下方句型）
【3. Qualitative note】（决策题加）← 一句"还需考虑非财务因素"
```

## 四、各题型现成结论句型（填数字即用）

### 本量利 CVP
- "The unit contribution margin is **$＿** (= selling price − variable cost)."
- "The break-even point is **＿ units** (or **$＿**), computed as fixed costs ÷ unit contribution margin."
- "To earn a target profit of $＿, the company must sell **＿ units**."

### 高低点法
- "Using the high-low method, variable cost per unit is **$＿** and fixed cost is **$＿**; the cost function is **Y = ＿ + ＿X**."

### 特殊订单
- "The company **should accept / should not accept** the special order because it provides an incremental contribution margin of **$＿**."

### 自制或外购
- "The company **should make / should buy** because the relevant cost of ＿ is **$＿ lower** than the alternative."

### 联产品深加工
- "The product **should be sold at the split-off point / processed further** because incremental revenue (**$＿**) is **less than / greater than** the separable cost (**$＿**). Joint costs are **irrelevant**."

### 设备更新
- "The old equipment **should be replaced / kept** because total relevant costs are **$＿ lower** under that option. The book value of the old equipment is a **sunk cost** and is irrelevant."

### 预算
- "Total cash collections for ＿ are **$＿**." / "The ending cash balance is **$＿**, which is **above / below** the minimum required balance, so **no borrowing / borrowing of $＿** is needed."

### 业绩评价
- "The division's **ROI is ＿%** and its **residual income is $＿**."
- "Under ROI the project would be **rejected**, but under residual income it should be **accepted** because its return exceeds the cost of capital."

### 资本预算
- "The **net present value is $＿**; since it is **positive / negative**, the project **should be accepted / rejected**."
- "The **payback period is ＿ years**." / "The **IRR is ＿%**, which **exceeds / is below** the required rate of return."

### 概念简答（高频）
- 沉没成本："A **sunk cost** is a past cost that cannot be changed by any current or future decision; therefore it is **irrelevant** to decision making."
- 相关成本："**Relevant costs** are **future** costs that **differ** among alternatives."
- 机会成本："An **opportunity cost** is the contribution to income that is forgone by **not** using a resource in its best alternative use."
- 平衡计分卡："The **balanced scorecard** measures performance from four perspectives: **financial, customer, internal business process, and learning & growth.**"
- 质量成本："Costs of quality are classified as **prevention, appraisal, internal failure, and external failure** costs."

## 五、一份完整的"全英作答"示范（照着学怎么排版）

> **Question:** ABC Co. normally sells 10,000 units at $25 each. Unit variable cost is $15 and total fixed cost is $60,000. ABC has idle capacity. A customer offers to buy **1,500 units at $18 each**. **Should ABC accept the special order? Show your analysis.**

**Answer:**

**1. Analysis (incremental approach):**

| | Per unit | 1,500 units |
|---|---:|---:|
| Incremental sales revenue | $18 | $27,000 |
| Incremental variable cost | $15 | $22,500 |
| **Incremental contribution margin** | **$3** | **$4,500** |
| Incremental fixed cost | — | $0 |
| **Increase in operating income** | | **$4,500** |

**2. Decision:** ABC **should accept** the special order because it increases operating income by **$4,500**, and total fixed costs do not change. (There is idle capacity, so regular sales are not affected.)

**3. Qualitative note:** ABC should also consider whether accepting a lower price ($18 vs. $25) might upset regular customers or invite future requests for discounts.

> 看到没？**表格 + 一句 Decision + 一句 qualitative**，全英作答就完成了。把数字和方案名换成你题目里的即可。

## 六、数字与格式的英文规范（小但易丢分）

- 货币写 `$`，金额用千分位：`$1,200,000`。
- **负数/流出**用括号：`$(3,000)` 表示 −3,000。
- "每单位"= **per unit**；"每年"= **per year / annually**；"百万/千"= million / thousand。
- 百分比写 `%`：`40%`。
- 结论里方案动词固定：**accept / reject, make / buy, keep / replace, sell / process further, increase / decrease**。

## 七、考前 5 分钟英文急救包（背这几个就够开口）

```
Contribution margin = Sales − Variable costs
Break-even = Fixed costs ÷ unit contribution margin
Relevant = future + differs among alternatives
Sunk cost = irrelevant
The company should accept/reject because ... increases/decreases operating income by $___.
NPV is positive → accept.
```
