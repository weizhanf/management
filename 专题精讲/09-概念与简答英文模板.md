## A. 简答题英文模板（高频考点，照背）

> 作答技巧：用 **要点式(bullet)** 或 **2~4 句**，含关键英文术语即可得分。

### A1. 管理会计的定义与作用 ★最高频（老师点名）
> "Management accounting is the process of **identifying, measuring, and reporting financial and non-financial information** that helps **internal managers make decisions**. It serves three main roles, answering:
> (1) **Scorecard questions** — evaluating organizational performance;
> (2) **Attention-directing questions** — comparing actual with expected results to identify problems and opportunities;
> (3) **Problem-solving questions** — evaluating alternative courses of action."

### A2. 管理会计 vs 财务会计
> "Management accounting is **future-oriented**, serves **internal managers**, reports on **segments** of the organization, and is **not bound by GAAP**. Financial accounting is **historical**, serves **external users** (investors, creditors), reports on the **whole company**, and **must follow GAAP**."

### A3. 相关信息 / 相关成本
> "**Relevant information** consists of **expected future** costs and revenues that **differ among alternatives**. Costs that have already been incurred (**sunk costs**) or that are the **same** under all alternatives are **irrelevant**."

### A4. 预算的作用与重要性（第7章）
> "A **budget** is a quantitative expression of a plan of action. Budgets are important because they:
> (1) **force managers to plan ahead**;
> (2) **promote coordination and communication** among departments;
> (3) **provide a benchmark** for evaluating performance; and
> (4) **motivate** managers and employees."

### A5. 责任会计与责任中心（第9章）
> "**Responsibility accounting** assigns revenues and costs to the manager who can **control** them. The four **responsibility centers** are: **cost center** (controls costs), **revenue center** (controls revenues), **profit center** (controls both — i.e., profit), and **investment center** (controls profit and invested capital). A manager should be evaluated only on **controllable** items."

### A6. 成本效益均衡 & 行为影响（第1章）
> "**Cost-benefit balance**: an accounting system should be adopted only if its **benefits exceed its costs**. **Behavioral implications**: accounting systems **influence the behavior and decisions of managers**, so measures must be designed to encourage goal-congruent behavior."

### A7. 价值链（第1章）
> "The **value chain** is the sequence of business functions that add value to a product: **research & development → product design → production → marketing → distribution → customer service**."

### A8. JIT / Lean / TQM（第1章）
> "**Just-in-time (JIT)** produces goods only as needed to **minimize inventory**. **Lean manufacturing** focuses on **eliminating waste** in all processes. **Total quality management (TQM)** is an organization-wide effort to **continuously improve quality**."

### A9. 弹性预算 vs 静态预算（第8章）
> "A **static (master) budget** is based on a **single planned** level of activity, whereas a **flexible budget** is **adjusted to the actual** level of activity, allowing a fair comparison of actual results with budgeted amounts at the same volume."

### A10. CVP 的假设（第2章，可能考简答）
> "CVP analysis assumes: costs can be separated into **fixed and variable**; total revenue and total cost are **linear** within the **relevant range**; **selling price, unit variable cost, and fixed costs are constant**; the **sales mix is constant**; and **inventory levels do not change** significantly."

### A11. 职业道德（第1章）
> "Management accountants must follow ethical standards of **competence, confidentiality, integrity, and credibility (objectivity)**."
